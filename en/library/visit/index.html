Our measures to prevent the spread of the COVID-19

  • Installation of alcohol-based hand sanitizer
  • Periodic cleaning and disinfection of common areas
  • Limiting the number of seats and terminals to ensure proper social distancing
  • Our staff take their temperature, frequently wash their hands, and wear masks.
  • Placement of partitions on the service counter
  • Ventilation by ventilation systems. (For protecting our materials, we do not leave the door and the windows open.)
